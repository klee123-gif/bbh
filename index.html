Explore Borneo
Discover the incredible life that makes Borneo one of Earth's richest biodiversity hotspots.
Rafflesia
No leaves, no roots, no stems, a parasitic lifestyle and a short blooming period. Because of these unique traits, the Rafflesia remains one of the most fascinating biological marvels in Borneo.
Borneon Orangutan
Every day, more trees fall. More orangutans are left stranded, confused, alone. To see one is to feel your heart break—because you know it may not have a tomorrow.
Sea Turtle
Borneo is home to two main species of sea turtles: green turtles and hawksbill turtles, both of which are endangered.
